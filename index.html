Utilizing the Oculus Quest, our solution will immerse the patient within a virtual environment that dynamically adapts to the stage of treatment, severity of the disorder, realtime integrated biosensor readings, and external inputs from a clinician and caregiver. With our product, we plan to provide an alternative, more effective treatment option for those who suffer from Agoraphobia and social anxiety disorders.
